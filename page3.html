Our Mission

Each day, thousands of people online save recipes from their favorite content creators. What if there was a website that brought attention to a variety of bakers and their recipes, regardless of how large their platform is?

Here at Sweets Across Tables, we tie up our aprons and learn recipes from different cultures. We do this so that everyday, more attention will be brought to these bakers and their delicious treats!


About The Author

Hello there! I’m Angela, a baker, designer, and full-time student. Since 2016, I've constantly been baking new desserts and sharing them with family, friends, and teachers. As an Asian home baker, I’m here to spread joy through baking and celebrating international flavors—one sweet treat at a time, using this website that I coded. I hope this website will help inspire others to try out new desserts and cultural dishes!
